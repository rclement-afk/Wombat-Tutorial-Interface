2026 Fall Game — Mission 12 of 18

12 Restack the Freight
SkillPrecise driving and articulating cubes into a stack.
Mission Components & Scoring
BASE MISSION
11 pts
Difficulty 6
⚡ Live Judged

Two spilled cubes form a scoring stack — one spilled cube is ON TOP OF another spilled cube. Both cubes must originate from the spilled cube area.

BONUS MISSION
5 pts
Difficulty 3
📋 Final Position

The scoring stack remains intact and the lower cube of the stack is touching black tape.

✓ Examples That Score
  • One spilled cube is ON TOP OF another spilled cube.
  • A robot is supporting one or both cubes while the ON TOP OF relationship exists.
  • The scoring stack exists and the lower cube is touching black tape.
✗ Examples That Do Not Score
  • Two spilled cubes touching side-by-side.
  • A spilled cube stacked on a cube that did not originate from the spilled cube area.
  • The stack no longer exists at final scoring.
  • The upper cube is touching black tape but the lower cube is not.
Judge Notes
  • The Base Mission is live scored.
  • The Bonus Mission is final position scored.
  • ON TOP OF is determined using the ON TOP OF definition.
  • Both cubes used for scoring must originate from the spilled cube area.
  • The stack must still exist at final scoring to receive Bonus Mission points.
  • The lower cube must be touching black tape to satisfy the Bonus Mission.
